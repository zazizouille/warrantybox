Privacy Policy for WarrantyBox

Last Updated: January 25, 2026

WarrantyBox ("we," "our," "us," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account information (email address, full name, phone number, country)
  • Warranty and receipt information (product details, purchase dates, warranty end dates, store names, item names, amounts, and notes)
  • Photos and documents you upload
  • Preferred currency and other user preferences

Automatically Collected Information

When you use our app, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Push notification tokens (for warranty expiration alerts)
  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • Usage data (features used, time spent in app)
  • Log data (access times, app errors)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Authenticate and manage your account
  • Store and organize your warranty and receipt information
  • Send you notifications about warranty expirations
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security concerns
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and requests
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities

3. App Permissions

WarrantyBox requires the following permissions:

  • Camera: To capture and upload receipt photos
  • Photo Library (iOS) / Storage (Android): To access photos and documents from your device
  • Notifications: To send you warranty expiration alerts

These permissions are only used for the purpose of capturing, storing, and managing receipt images and sending relevant notifications.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Supabase (PostgreSQL database) servers with the following security measures:

  • All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS)
  • Row-level security ensures only you can access your data
  • Data is encrypted and protected against unauthorized or unlawful processing
  • We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect against accidental loss, destruction, or damage

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations or government requests
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • With service providers who assist us in operating our app (under strict confidentiality agreements)

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase for database, authentication, and file storage
  • Expo for push notification delivery

Their privacy policies apply to their services. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Export your data
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time through the app settings

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time through the app settings. Upon deletion, your personal information will be removed from our active databases.

8. Children and Parental Responsibility

WarrantyBox is designed and marketed for adults managing receipts and warranties. While the app contains no content inherently harmful to children, we do not target marketing or features at children.

If children under 13 access the app under parental supervision, parents or guardians are responsible for:

  • Monitoring their activity
  • Ensuring data accuracy (children may inadvertently modify receipt or warranty information)
  • Understanding that this app is not a children's app and contains adult-oriented financial data

We do not knowingly market the app to or collect information specifically from children, but we acknowledge parental discretion in allowing supervised access.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
